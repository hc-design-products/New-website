Antler

Lamp of Bronze Crane with Antler

This lamp, a modern interpretation of ancient Chinese metal casting and inlaying techniques, showcases the beauty of antique design. Inspired by the excavated artefact known as the "Bronze Crane with Antler," the lamp simplifies and scales down the form factor to a size suitable for a table lamp.

Have a look at the product

With a 3D model, you can explore the form from different angles and examine every design detail. You can also use your smartphone to visit the website and try the AR mode, which lets you place the model in real-world settings.
