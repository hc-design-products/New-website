GEMZ

Sparkling loyalty and engagement - The dynamic reward staking APP

GEMZ ia a short project to design and develop a reward page, with a loyalty program that allows users to earn points and redeem rewards, including fine dining experiences, live sports event tickets, and music concert passes. To enhance user engagement, the reward page incorporates dynamic animations of gems stacking up, with 21 blocks representing each loyalty level.

About this project

  • My role : Individual
    User experience / User Interface/
  • Main tools involved
    Figma
  • Project duration
    1 week

User-Centric Approach

Persona : Understanding User Needs

Mark, is a 35-year-old tech-savvy IT Project Manager located in Chicago, IL. He enjoys dining out at fine restaurants, attending sports events, and going to concerts to discover new experiences. Mark values convenience and seeks meaningful rewards to enhance his lifestyle. He's proficient with technology but tends to be skeptical of apps, preferring to use his smartphone for essential tasks.

Key features

Considering the persona, GEMZ has been designed and developed with a user-centric, playful approach:

Dynamic Gem Stacking: The app features a visually engaging dynamic gem stacking animation that displays Mark's progress and rewards in a fun and interactive way.
Tailored Rewards: GEMZ offers a diverse range of rewards, including fine dining experiences, live sports tickets, and music event passes, aligning with Mark's interests. The interface is designed for easy navigation, ensuring that Mark can quickly find, track, and redeem rewards.
Status Levels: like Mark can achieve different status levels within the loyalty program, offering a sense of achievement and recognition.

Prototype Design

Multiple frames and animated components are incorporated to deliver a dynamic and engaging user experience, enhancing Mark's interaction with the app.

Outcome expectation

By tailoring the GEMZ app to address Mark's specific goals, motivations, challenges, and personality traits, we anticipate several positive outcomes:

Increased User Engagement: dynamic gem stacking animation, along with animated components, will capture Mark's attention and keep him engaged with the app.
Improved Loyalty: will find it easier to engage with the loyalty program, fostering a sense of loyalty and recognition.
Enhanced User Experience: app's streamlined interface, visual rewards tracking, and animations will make it more enjoyable for Mark to save money and discover new experiences.
Strong First Impression: addressing Mark's app loyalty tendencies, GEMZ will make a strong first impression and encourage him to stick with the app. This updated section emphasizes the use of animated components to enhance the user experience within the GEMZ app.
